### Gaps Summary

No automated gaps found. All five must-have truths are verified against actual code:

- `_handleStopTask` is fully rewritten to call `handleStopAutomation` directly without any `_isDashboardTask` filter.
- `handleStopAutomation` captures `lastAction` and `duration` before cleanup and returns them in `sendResponse`.
- The `_dashStopSent` dedup flag prevents `broadcastDashboardComplete` from sending a duplicate `ext:task-complete` after the stop handler has already sent one.
- `dashboard.js` tracks `lastProgressAction` through the full lifecycle (declared, assigned on progress, consumed on stop, reset on completion).
- The failed state in `setTaskState` now renders elapsed time via `formatDuration`.

The one informational finding (catch path does not clear `_dashboardTaskTabId`) does not block any goal truth and has no user-visible impact.
